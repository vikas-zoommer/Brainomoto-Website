The Missing Link

What is Learning Readiness?

Learning Readiness is the condition where a child's brain and body systems are mature enough to absorb, process, and retain new information effectively.

Without learning readiness, even the brightest children struggle. Traditional tutoring addresses symptoms, not the root cause.

Research shows: "Learning readiness contributes highly to educational achievement. Absence of learning readiness spoils the teaching-learning process."
